Privacy Policy for Pete Desk Assistant

Last updated: May 2025

Pete Desk Assistant is a voice-based personal assistant that runs entirely on your local device (such as a Raspberry Pi).

Data Collection

The assistant does not collect or store any personal data. All voice recognition, reminders, and command processing happens locally on your device.

When you authorize access to your Google Calendar, your calendar data is used temporarily to read upcoming events, and is never stored or shared.

Data Usage

  • Google Calendar data is used only to respond to your voice query (e.g., “what’s on my calendar?”).
  • No data is sent to any external server, third-party, or cloud service other than Google APIs required to access your calendar.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the Google Calendar API. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.
